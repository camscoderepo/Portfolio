About Me

My name is Cameron Roman

I live in Denver, Colorado. I am a very passionate and self-taught web developer seeking employment opportunities. I have experience in making apps for both, myself and real world clients alike. I thrive on quality work and always will go the extra mile. That is what makes my work special and stand out. I have a large skill set that includes experience with javascript, C#, Python, PHP, Microsoft SQL Server, MongoDB, Node.js, React, HTML5, and CSS3. Most of my github projects have been written in javascript, which is my favorite language to work in.

My interests other than programming include video games, Netflix binges with my girlfriend, lounging with my cat, game design, game development, and animation. I aspire to work in the games industry one day, though I realize I will have to figure out how to stand out in the competitive market.
